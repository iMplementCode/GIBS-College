Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and enrollment process

What are the admission requirements?

+

We welcome students from all backgrounds! Basic requirements include a high school diploma or equivalent, basic computer literacy, and a passion for technology. No prior programming experience is required for beginner courses.

How long are the courses?

+

Course durations vary from 4 to 6 months depending on the program. We offer both full-time and part-time schedules to accommodate working professionals.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

+

Yes! We have a dedicated career services team that provides resume reviews, interview preparation, and connections to our network of hiring partners. 95% of our graduates find employment within 3 months.

What is the fee structure and payment options?

+

We offer flexible payment plans including full payment discounts, monthly installments, and scholarship opportunities. Contact our admissions team for detailed fee information and available financial aid options.

Can I study while working?

+

Absolutely! Our evening and weekend classes are specifically designed for working professionals. We also offer online and hybrid learning options for maximum flexibility.

What technologies and tools will I learn?

+

Our curriculum covers industry-standard technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, SQL, Git, and more. You'll work with the same tools used by top tech companies worldwide.
